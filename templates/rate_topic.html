Evaluation Guidelines

Please evaluate the hypotheses based on the following 5 dimensions:

  • Novelty/Originality: Which proposal presents a more surprising, non-obvious, or groundbreaking idea?
  • Soundness/Rationality: Which proposal is more theoretically and logically sound? Consider the clarity of the writing as a prerequisite for soundness. Which one is built on more solid assumptions?
  • Feasibility: Which proposal has a more practical and scientifically valid experiment plan? Which one is more likely to be successfully executed in a real-world setting?
  • Significance/Impact: If successful, which idea would have a more profound and lasting influence on the field? Which one tackles a more important problem?
  • Overall Winner: After weighing your judgments across all four dimensions, make a holistic decision and declare an overall winner.
